# Contract Review Checklist

## Basic Information

- Are both parties clearly identified?
- Are names, addresses, contact information, ID numbers, or unified social credit codes included?

## Subject Matter

- Is the service, product, lease object, or work result clearly described?
- Are deliverables defined?

## Price and Payment

- Is the total price clear?
- Is payment time clear?
- Are installments, invoices, taxes, and payment methods clear?

## Performance and Delivery

- Is the delivery or performance deadline clear?
- Are milestones defined?
- Are acceptance criteria defined?

## Breach Liability

- Is there a clear breach clause?
- Is liquidated damage calculable?
- Is compensation scope clear?
- Are both parties' liabilities balanced?

## Dispute Resolution

- Is litigation or arbitration specified?
- Is jurisdiction clear?
- Is governing law clear?

## Termination

- Are termination conditions specified?
- Are refund, settlement, and handover rules specified?

## Special Clauses

For software or website development contracts:

- Is source code delivery specified?
- Is intellectual property ownership specified?
- Is maintenance period specified?
- Is requirement change procedure specified?

For lease contracts:

- Is rent payment specified?
- Is maintenance responsibility specified?
- Is sublease rule specified?

For labor-related agreements:

- Are work content, workplace, compensation, working time, and social insurance specified?
